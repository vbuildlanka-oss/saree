A deep madder-dyed Kanjivaram with a contrast pallu and real gold zari woven along the border. Four months on a single loom, signed by its weaver. One of eight.

Colour Maroon
Fabric Pure Silk

Rani is woven from pure mulberry silk, dyed with madder root for its characteristic depth. The contrast pallu carries a traditional temple border in genuine gold zari. Because each saree is dyed in a small batch, slight variation in tone is a signature of the hand, not a flaw.

Pure Kanjivaram mulberry silk · genuine gold-dipped zari · 6.3m length + blouse piece · approx. 780g.

Dry clean only. Store folded in muslin, away from light. Insured shipping worldwide; 7-day returns on unworn pieces.
